========================================
NEXUS STREETWEAR WEBSITE - EDITING GUIDE
========================================

HOW TO EDIT YOUR WEBSITE:

1. OPEN THE FILE
   - Right-click on "index.html" 
   - Select "Open with" > "Notepad" (or any text editor)

2. WHAT YOU CAN EDIT:

   A) PRODUCT NAMES
      - Find: <h3 class="product-name">Classic Hoodie</h3>
      - Change "Classic Hoodie" to your product name
      - Do this for all 6 products

   B) PRODUCT PRICES
      - Find: <p class="product-price">$79.99</p>
      - Change "$79.99" to your price
      - Do this for all 6 products

   C) PRODUCT IMAGES
      - Find: <img src="product1.jpg" alt="Product 1">
      - Put your image files in the NexusStreetwear folder
      - Name them: product1.jpg, product2.jpg, etc.
      - OR change "product1.jpg" to match your image filename

   D) HERO SECTION TEXT
      - Find: <p class="hero-subtitle">Premium Quality. Unmatched Style.</p>
      - Change the text between > and </p>

   E) ABOUT SECTION
      - Find the section that starts with: <section id="about"
      - Change the paragraph text to describe your brand

   F) CONTACT INFORMATION
      - Find: Email: info@nexusstreetwear.com
      - Change to your real email
      - Find: Phone: (555) 123-4567
      - Change to your real phone number

3. SAVE YOUR CHANGES
   - Press Ctrl+S to save
   - Or go to File > Save

4. VIEW YOUR CHANGES
   - Double-click index.html to open in browser
   - Refresh the page (press F5) to see updates

========================================
QUICK TIPS:
========================================

- Use Ctrl+F to search for text you want to change
- Be careful not to delete the < > symbols or quotes
- Always save before viewing in browser
- Keep a backup copy before making big changes

========================================
ADDING YOUR PRODUCT IMAGES:
========================================

1. Get your product photos ready
2. Resize them to around 800x800 pixels (optional but recommended)
3. Save them as JPG files
4. Name them: product1.jpg, product2.jpg, product3.jpg, etc.
5. Put them in the same folder as index.html
6. Refresh your website in the browser

========================================
NEED HELP?
========================================

If something breaks:
- Check that you didn't delete any < > symbols
- Make sure all quotes " " are still there
- Look for the original text in this guide and compare

Common mistakes:
- Deleting </h3> or </p> tags
- Removing quotes around filenames
- Breaking the HTML structure
